# Brand Voice Guidelines

## Brand Archetype

**Magician** / Sage

Smoke Signals AI embodies the Magician archetype by transforming complex AI and GTM challenges into innovative, strategic solutions that create powerful, almost 'magical' results for clients. The brand also carries a strong Sage influence through its emphasis on knowledge, foresight, and strategic innovation in the AI marketing space.

### Key Traits
- innovative
- transformative
- strategic
- visionary
- knowledgeable

### Tone Attributes
- casual
- serious
- respectful
- enthusiastic

## Tone Description

The brand voice is casual yet serious, blending respectful professionalism with enthusiastic confidence. It speaks with clarity and purpose, grounded in deep knowledge and visionary insight. Messaging balances approachable language with strategic depth, inspiring trust and excitement about AI’s transformative potential in GTM.

## Voice Principles


### Transformative Clarity

Communicate complex AI and GTM concepts in a clear, insightful way that feels almost magical in revealing new possibilities.

**How to Apply**: Use vivid metaphors and precise explanations to illustrate how AI transforms marketing systems, ensuring messaging is accessible yet intellectually stimulating.

> **Example**: "We design winning GTM systems that make AI actually perform — turning chaos into signal-driven precision in 90 days."

**What NOT to Do**: Avoid jargon-heavy or overly technical language that confuses or alienates the audience.


### Strategic Visionary

Convey a forward-thinking, knowledgeable perspective rooted in deep expertise and anticipation of future trends.

**How to Apply**: Frame messaging around foresight and innovative strategy, highlighting how the brand anticipates and shapes AI’s evolving impact on marketing.

> **Example**: "Nick has demonstrated his ability to anticipate tech trends and think ahead of the curve for years... He embodies strategic innovation."

**What NOT to Do**: Do not sound reactive, vague, or uncertain about the direction and potential of AI in GTM.


### Respectful Enthusiasm

Maintain a tone that is casual and approachable yet serious, respectful, and genuinely enthusiastic about the brand’s mission and client success.

**How to Apply**: Use conversational language with a professional edge, balancing enthusiasm with respect for the audience’s intelligence and challenges.

> **Example**: "You can’t throw a rock without hitting 10 new AI-enabled GTM tools. Finding strategy? Good luck. Or just call Nick and Smoke Signals."

**What NOT to Do**: Avoid sarcasm, irreverence, or overly casual slang that could undermine credibility or alienate professional audiences.


## Vocabulary

Use precise, strategic, and engaging language that reflects innovation and expertise while remaining accessible and inspiring confidence.

### Words to Use
- transform
- signal-driven
- innovative
- strategic
- anticipate
- refine
- visionary
- engage

### Words to Avoid
- just
- maybe
- try
- cheap

## Cadence

Sentences are generally concise and dynamic, mixing short, impactful statements with slightly longer, explanatory phrases. Rhythm is purposeful, with a flow that guides readers through complex ideas smoothly. Avoid overly dense blocks of text; instead, use varied sentence lengths and structure to maintain engagement and clarity.
